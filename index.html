MECA - Middle Eastern Children's Alliance

Nonprofit Website Redesign

OVERVIEW
MECA is a nonprofit organization working for the rights and the well-being of children in the Middle East. They work to direct aid, clean water, financial support for community projects, and university scholarships.
The primary goal of the website rediesgn for MECA is to create a more engaging, informative, and user-friendly digital presence that supports their mission of aiding children in the Middle East and enhances their ability to reach and connnect with their audience effectively.
TEAM
Sonia Halmi
Zeyna Jama
Rupali Kakhanpal
TIME FRAME
2 Weeks
ROLE
UI/UX Designer
User Research and Analysis, Information Architecture, Visual design, Wireframing, Prototyping & Testing

PHASE ONE: DISCOVER

To begin, we needed to determine:
  • What are the main reasons users go onto the website?
  • What catches users attention when first opening the website.
  • How could it be improved?
To answer these questions, we:
  • Constructed a short survey to gather data on how users feel about nonprofit websites.
  • Interviewed 5 users who actively donate to different nonprofits.
  • Performed a heuristic evaluation on the current website to determine what keeps users from finding the information they are looking for and any pain points they might run into.
